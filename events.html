2022 Winter Festival

Friday December 16 3:45-5:45pm @ Pasadena High School

2925 E Sierra Madre Blvd, Pasadena, CA 91107


The first annual 28% Winter Festival was a success!

We were able to raise funds to cover printing/event costs by selling clay jewelry, holiday cards, and crochet items!

Drama club had a hot chocolate booth, SAGA brought delicious baked goods, TacoBell was provided by PopPay, Guitar Club played us all some Christmas Carols, and The 28% hosted a raffle for three $50 giftcards!

It was a lot of fun! Thank you to everyone who stopped by - see you all next year!!
